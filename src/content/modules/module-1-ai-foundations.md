This module covers the foundational AI knowledge you need before you can guard AI systems. You will learn how neural networks learn, how large language models generate text, how models are trained and aligned, and how production AI applications are assembled.

A guardrail engineer does not need to train models, but they must understand the technology deeply enough to know *where* vulnerabilities come from and *why* certain guardrail strategies work while others fail. Every concept in this module connects directly to a guardrail decision you will face in later modules.
