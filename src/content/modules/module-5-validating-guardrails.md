This module covers the complete validation lifecycle — from adversarial testing before deployment to ongoing monitoring in production. You will learn red teaming methodology, testing strategies, evaluation metrics, observability patterns, and lifecycle management.

Guardrails that are not rigorously validated are security theater. This module ensures you can prove your guardrails work, measure their effectiveness with real metrics, detect when they degrade, and respond when they fail. Validation is not a phase — it is a continuous practice that runs for as long as the AI system is in production.
