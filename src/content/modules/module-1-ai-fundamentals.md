**Domain Weight:** 15% of exam
**Estimated Study Time:** 3-4 hours

The prerequisites gave you the technical foundation — how LLMs work, how they are trained, and why the architecture creates risk. This module applies that foundation to guardrail engineering. Section 1.1 identifies the architectural properties that drive guardrail design and shows where controls can be placed. Section 1.2 catalogs the failure modes those controls must address. Section 1.3 teaches you to systematically identify threats and prioritize your guardrail investment. By the end, you will have the vocabulary, the failure taxonomy, and the threat modeling skills that the rest of the program builds on.
