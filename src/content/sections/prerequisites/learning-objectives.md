After completing this module, you will be able to:

- Describe how neural networks learn patterns through the training loop (forward pass, loss, backpropagation, weight update)
- Explain the transformer architecture, self-attention, and why it replaced sequential models
- Trace the complete data path through an LLM from text input to token generation
- Describe each stage of LLM training and the capabilities and risks each stage introduces
- Map architectural properties of LLMs to the specific risks they create
- Identify trust boundaries in AI systems and explain the defense-in-depth principle
